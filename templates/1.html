Skin Cancer Image Classification using CNNs: A Comparative Analysis





About

Cancer is the most threatening of them all. Among all the variants of cancer, skin cancer is spreading rapidly. It happens because of the abnormal growth of skin cells.

Benign and malignant types are the most common skin cancers people suffer from. People go through expensive and time-consuming treatments to cure skin cancer but yet fail to lower the mortality rate.

The convolutional neural network (CNN) helps to find skin cancer through image classification more accurately. This research contains information about many CNN models and a comparison of their working processes for finding the best results. Pretrained models like VGG16, ResNet50 and etc.,

A dataset from Kaggle is used, which is named “‘Skin Cancer’ Malignant vs. Benign”. Kaggle is one of the top resources for data scientists and machine learners looking for datasets.
